Build a web application for “The Foundation” powered by LPT Realty, with an integrated AI chatbot called **S2**.

This is not a generic real estate website.
It should feel calm, selective, useful, and structured.

Tone:

* No hype
* No pressure
* Human
* Direct

---

## CORE POSITION

This platform exists to help agents:

* reduce unnecessary cost
* use better tools properly
* build a more consistent business

Not to recruit them.
Not to convince them.

If they improve, everything else follows.

---

## APP STRUCTURE

Create these routes:

* `/`
* `/leadership`
* `/realtors`
* `/transition`
* `/s2`
* `/contact`

---

## HOME ROUTE

Purpose:

Introduce The Foundation clearly.

Requirements:

* clean minimal layout
* short grounded positioning statement
* two clear paths:

  * Leadership
  * Realtors

Also make visible that **S2 is a chatbot** and can be accessed at any time for practical questions.

---

## LEADERSHIP ROUTE

Behavior:

* page loads with blurred / distorted background
* centered modal asks for password

Password: `Founders`

If password is correct:

* remove blur with smooth fade
* reveal full content

If password is incorrect:

* small shake animation
* subtle invalid state

Content focus:

* why they were approached
* this is about building markets properly
* not for everyone
* looking for the right people
* quiet invitation to start a conversation

S2 can also be referenced here as available for follow-up questions.

---

## REALTORS ROUTE

Purpose:

Speak to working agents who want better structure.

Important:

Do NOT emphasize recruiting.
Do NOT emphasize revenue share.

The page should communicate:

* save money
* better tools
* real usage of those tools
* more consistency
* more stable business growth

Core logic:

As agents improve their actual business:

* their work becomes more consistent
* their results improve
* their business becomes more stable

This becomes visible.

People around them notice.

Conversations may happen naturally later, but that is not the focus of this page.

Position clearly:

Agents do not need to recruit.
They need to improve.

Everything else follows.

Page sections:

1. grounded explanation section
2. calculator module
3. practical tool usage section
4. calm CTA

Also mention that **S2 the chatbot** is available for questions about tools, onboarding, and day-to-day workflow.

---

## CALCULATOR MODULE

Create a simple interactive calculator.

Inputs:

* deals per year
* commission split
* annual cap
* monthly fees
* transaction fees
* franchise / royalty fee

Outputs:

* estimated current brokerage cost
* estimated LPT cost
* estimated savings

Keep the UI simple and readable.

---

## TRANSITION ROUTE

Purpose:

Make brokerage transition feel manageable.

Render 4 steps:

1. Prepare
2. Exit
3. Onboard
4. Launch

Each step should have:

* short explanatory text
* clean layout
* practical tone

Also make clear that **S2** can answer transition questions at any stage.

---

## S2 ROUTE

Position clearly:

S2 is a chatbot.

Description:

S2 is the digital version of Stu.
Direct, useful, grounded.

S2 should be designed as a real working assistant, not a demo.

It should be presented as able to help with:

* LPT questions
* onboarding
* CRM usage
* clause writing guidance
* Ontario paperwork questions
* OREA / RECO basics
* general day-to-day workflow questions

UI requirements:

* chatbot interface
* clear message area
* input field
* clean styling
* easy access from all pages

Optional:

* floating chat button visible site-wide

---

## CONTACT ROUTE

Simple page with:

Tone should remain calm and low pressure.
